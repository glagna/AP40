🧬 MHC I vs MHC II Decision Game

BIOL 40C · Wk 6

Goal: For each cell-scenario, decide (1) which MHC class displays the antigen, (2) which T cell responds, and (3) what happens. The two-rule cheat sheet from class:

MHC I = "what I made"  ·  on every nucleated cell  ·  read by CD8+ cytotoxic T cells → kill the cell.

MHC II = "what I ate"  ·  only on antigen-presenting cells (dendritic, macrophage, B cell)  ·  read by CD4+ helper T cells → license other immune cells.
